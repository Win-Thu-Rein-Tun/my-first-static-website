Privacy Policy

Personal Data Protection is important to us


This Privacy Policy applies to the trinet.com.sg website (the “Website”) and governs data collection, processing and usage in compliance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (No. 26 of 2012) of Singapore (“PDPA”). By using the Website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.


1. Personal data


1.1 Personal data is defined in the PDPA as “data, whether true or not, about an individual who can be identified - from that data; or from that data and other information to which the organisation has or is likely to have access”. which includes the below but not limited to :


  • Name, gender, date of birth, passport or other personal identification numbers, images, photographs, videos, closed circuit television (CCTV) footage, voice recordings;
  • Contact information e.g., address, phone number, email address;
  • Payment information e.g., credit or debit card information, including the name of cardholder, card number, billing address and expiry date;
  • Bio metric information;
  • Health information e.g., medical records or requests; and
  • Technical information e.g., IP address or device identification.

1.2 The types of data that the Trinet collects depends on the circumstances of the collection and on the nature of the service requested or transaction undertaken.


1.3 If you choose to make an enquiry or an application , send us an email, approach us in person or through any of our various offices in Singapore and overseas, we may require you to provide contact and identity information and other personal data (including name, address, contact details). Where possible, we have indicated the fields which are required and the fields which are optional.


2. Collection and Retention of Personal Data / Information


2.1 Generally, Trinet collects Personal Data either directly from you or your authorized representatives (i.e. persons who have been validly identified as being authorized by you through our security procedures) in the following ways :


  • Submit forms relating to or purchase or use any of our products or services;
  • Interact with our customer service officers, including via telephone calls, face-to-face meetings and emails;
  • Request that we contact you or be included in an email or other mailing list;
  • Respond to our promotions or other incentives;
  • Respond to our request for additional Personal Data;
  • Respond to our surveys;
  • Receive references from business partners and third parties, for example, where you have been referred by them;
  • Submit your Personal Data to us for any other reasons; and
  • Browse our website. If you are only browsing our website, you generally do so anonymously. We do not capture any data that allows us to identify you individually, unless you provide such information. However, we do use cookies on our websites. To find out more, please refer to the section on cookies below.

2.2 We collect , use , disclose and / or share your personal information for the following purposes


  • To communicate with you on any queries you may have;
  • To process your order and deliver products and services to you;
  • To manage the administration and business operations and complying with our policies and procedures
  • For service improvements, resolving complaints and disputes
  • For identity verification purposes;
  • To update our records and generally maintain your accounts with us;
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activity;
  • To meet or comply with any applicable rules, laws, regulations, codes of practice or guidelines issued by any legal or regulatory bodies which are binding on Trinet Technologies Pte Ltd (including but not limited to responding to regulatory complaints, disclosing to regulatory bodies and conducting audit checks, due diligence and investigations); and
  • purposes which are reasonably related to the aforesaid.

2.3 We will use all commercially reasonable endeavors to ensure that these third parties comply with similar undertakings of privacy.


  • With your consent: We may seek your consent to collect, hold, use and disclose your personal data for any other purpose not set out herein.
  • Business Transfers: As we continue to develop our business, we might sell or buy businesses, subsidiaries or business units or undertake a merger. In such transactions, customer information may be one of the transferred business assets.

2.4 Information collected by cookies


  • When you visit this website, our company servers automatically records information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website. This information may include:
  • Your computer’s IP address
  • Browser type
  • Websites visited before our website
  • Pages visited within our website (including time spent on those pages)
  • Other items and information searched for on our website, access times and dates etc
  • This information is collected for analysis and evaluation in order to help us improve this website, and the services and products we provide. This information will not be used in association with any personal data.
